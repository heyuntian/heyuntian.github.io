Introduction to the Honors Youth Program @ Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU)

The Honors Youth Program at XJTU (a.k.a. the Special Class for the Gifted Young) is an undergraduate program launched in 1985. In each year, about 100 talented teenagers under the age of 15 are admitted to this program. Click Here for more details about this program.
